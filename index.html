Hello! I am a first-year PhD student at the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Dr. Carolyn Rose. My interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, AI for Code and Human-AI collaboration. Specifically, I am interested in optimizing AI to be a more effective collaborative partner in tasks including programming or education, with attention to the nuances of human-AI interaction.

Previously, I worked as a research fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS), advised by Dr. Adish Singla, where I focused on improving how language models generate feedback for code.
