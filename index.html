Hello! I am a first-year PhD student at the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Dr. Carolyn Rose. I work at the intersection of NLP and AI for Code to improve human-AI collaboration, with applications in software development, education, and beyond.

Previously, I worked as a research fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS), advised by Dr. Adish Singla, where I focused on improving how language models generate feedback for code.
